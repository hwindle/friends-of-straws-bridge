Upcoming Events

Bat Walk

Date and time TBC

We hope to host our annual bat walk at the end of the summer. Join us for a walk around the Straw's Bridge nature reserve and nearby footpaths to see what bats we can see and hear on our bat detectors! A limited number of bat detectors will be available during the event - please return them after!

Next Meeting

The next meeting will take place at Legs Wine Bar, on Tuesday 26th September 2023 at 7.30pm. Legs Wine bar is found at 24 South Street, Ilkeston, DE7 5QE. There is a council car park around the corner on Queen Street which is free after 6pm
