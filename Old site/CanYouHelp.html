The Friends of Straw's Bridge would like to know how deep the main pond is - and what might be lurking below. In the 1980's Straw's Bridge was an opencast mining site. We know that the excavation was deep enough to have a roadway down for trucks and that some vehicles or machinery might have been left in the hole.
 
If you worked on the site or have any information or photographs we would like you to get in touch at friendsofstrawsbridge@erewash.gov.uk
